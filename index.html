About Me

Music Composer & Producer (Films, Advertisements, Company and
Foundation Profiles and also Solo / Band Album or Single) also Drummer for
Indonesian band SORE

Music Composer

As experienced music composer & producer, especially in the Indonesian
movie industry since 2005, I already composed music for more than 70
movies and hundreds of music for advertisements with my team
"Rooftopsound", nominated a few times and won "Best Music" for Festival
Film Indonesia 2017 & Piala Maya 2019. I also was a Music Director for
Musical Performance "Onrop Musikal" 2010 & "I Slank U, A Journey To The
Blue Island Concert" 2012.

Drumer of SORE

My band SORE is a well known band in Indonesia with several influentials
albums like "Centralismo", "Ports Of Lima", "Los Skut Leboys", several mini
albums and featured in many Indonesian Original Soundtracks Albums such
as "Berbagi Suami", "Pintu Terlarang", "Arisan 2", "Modus Anomali", and many
more. SORE also nominated by TIME MAGAZINE ASIA as "5 Asian Albums
Worth Buying", one of "Best Album Of Time" & one of "Best Indonesian
Drummer Of All Time" by ROLLING STONE INDONESIA Magazine
